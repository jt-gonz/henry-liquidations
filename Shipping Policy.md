# **Shipping & Delivery Policy**

**Last updated:** February 2026  
Thank you for visiting and shopping at **Henry's Liquidation Store**. Following are the terms and conditions that constitute our Shipping Policy.

## **1\. Processing Time**

All orders are processed within **2-3 business days**. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays.  
If we are experiencing a high volume of orders, shipments may be delayed by a few days. Please allow additional days in transit for delivery. If there will be a significant delay in shipment of your order, we will contact you via email or telephone.

## **2\. Shipping Rates & Delivery Estimates**

Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout based on the weight, dimensions, and destination of the furniture items.

| Shipping Method | Estimated Delivery Time | Cost |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **Local Delivery** (Within 20 miles) | 1-3 Business Days | **Free** |
| **In-Store Pickup** | Ready in 24 hours | **Free** |

*Delivery delays can occasionally occur.*

### **Freight Shipping (Large Items)**

Due to the size and weight of our furniture inventory, most items are shipped via freight carriers.

* **Curbside Delivery:** Standard shipping is "Curbside," meaning the carrier will drop the item off at the curb or end of your driveway. They will **not** bring the item inside your home or assemble it.  
* **Scheduling:** The carrier will typically call you 24 hours in advance to schedule a delivery window. Someone must be present to sign for the delivery.

## **3\. Local Delivery**

We offer local delivery for customers located within a **20 mile radius** of our warehouse in **Lafayette, Indiana**.

* Local delivery options will appear at checkout if your zip code is within our service area.  
* Our local team will contact you to schedule a drop-off time.

## **4\. In-Store Pickup**

You can skip the shipping fees with free local pickup at **1355 Sagamore Pkwy N, Lafayette, IN 47904**. After placing your order and selecting local pickup at checkout, your order will be prepared and ready for pick up within **1 to 2 business days**. We will send you an email when your order is ready along with instructions.  
**Pickup Hours:**

* Monday \- Friday: 10 AM \- 7 PM  
* Saturday \- Sunday: 10 AM \- 6 PM

*Please bring your order confirmation email and a valid photo ID.*

## **5\. Shipment to P.O. boxes or APO/FPO addresses**

Henry's Liquidation Store **cannot** ship furniture to P.O. boxes or APO/FPO addresses due to carrier restrictions on large items. Please provide a physical street address for delivery.

## **6\. Shipment Confirmation & Order Tracking**

You will receive a Shipment Confirmation email once your order has shipped containing your tracking number(s). The tracking number will be active within 24 hours.

## **7\. Customs, Duties and Taxes**

Henry's Liquidation Store is not responsible for any customs and taxes applied to your order. All fees imposed during or after shipping are the responsibility of the customer (tariffs, taxes, etc.).

## **8\. Damages**

Henry's Liquidation Store is not liable for any products damaged or lost during shipping. If you received your order damaged, please contact the shipment carrier to file a claim.

* **Important:** Please save all packaging materials and damaged goods before filing a claim.  
* **Inspection:** We highly recommend inspecting all freight shipments **upon delivery** before signing the bill of lading. If there is visible damage to the box, please note "DAMAGED" on the paperwork or refuse the shipment.

## **9\. International Shipping Policy**

We currently do not ship outside the United States.
